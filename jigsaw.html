Flowers Jigsaw Activity

Learners Description: This activity is made for all ages.

Overview

In this activity you will work in a group of five students. Each of you will look at one type of flower and become an “expert” on everything about it. After becoming an expert, you will present and teach the class about your type of flower.

Directions

  1. Break into groups of five and have each person pick a topic.
  2. Use the table below to pick which flower you will be looking at.
  3. Use the resource links to study your type of flower.
  4. While reading, take notes on your type of flower to use later.
  5. Next, join a group composed of other people with the same type of flower. Discuss the type of flower and answer any questions.
  6. Lastly, return to original groups. Each student will take turns teaching about their type of flower to their group. Make sure to include examples and have everyone share what they learned when you are done presenting your type of flower.
  7. Once each group is done, everyone will take the quiz on different types of flowers.
