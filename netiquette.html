Emailing Netiquette

Remember: Once you click send, it is out in the world forever.

Be Polite

  • Check Your Messages Often
  • Choose A Meaningful Subject

Be Respectful

  • Use Email The Way You Want Others To Use It
  • Consider Courtesy & Privacy

Be Formal

  • Get To The Point
  • Check Yor Speling, Grammer and Punctuation
  • Read Your Messages Before Sending
